About the Studio

Where precision meets purpose.

Move Fitness is a premium reformer Pilates studio nestled in the heart of Mont Kiara. We offer small group classes, private sessions, and curated wellness programmes designed for real results — not just aesthetics.

Our reformers are state-of-the-art Balanced Body equipment. Our instructors are internationally certified and hold themselves — and you — to the highest standards.
